# **App Name**: EmotiWell

## Core Features:

- Emotion Input: Allow users to input and express their current emotional state through simple and intuitive interfaces.
- Personalized Guidance: Provide users with calming techniques, coping strategies, emotional validation, and gentle encouragement tailored to their expressed emotional state.
- Self-Reflection Prompts: Offer daily prompts that encourage users to reflect on their feelings, triggers, and coping mechanisms to build self-awareness.
- Progress Tracking: Enable users to track their emotional states over time to identify patterns, improvements, and areas needing more support.
- AI Chatbot tool: A conversational AI tool provides users a friendly space to practice emotion regulation.
- Resource Directory: Offer a directory of mental health resources, including links to professional services, support groups, and educational materials.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft lavender (#E6E6FA) to evoke calmness and tranquility.
- Background color: Light grey (#F5F5F5) for a clean, non-intrusive backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted teal (#A7DBD8) to offer a sense of reassurance and stability, and act as highlights
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' for readability and a modern yet approachable feel. This sans-serif provides a gentle appearance conducive to a calming digital environment.
- Use gentle, rounded icons in pastel colors to represent different emotions and activities. These icons should be easily understandable and contribute to a welcoming atmosphere.
- Design a clean, spacious layout that is easy to navigate. Use white space effectively to avoid overwhelming users with information.
- Incorporate subtle animations for transitions and interactions to add a gentle, engaging feel without distracting from the app's calming purpose.
